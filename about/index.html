    This is our story – the who, what, where, when and why

    WHO

    The principals of Resonance have more than a half-century of experience helping companies tell stories about their purpose and ideas, about people and places, products and passions. Our team of strategists, writers, designers, videographers and web experts are devoted to bringing brand stories to life in every medium, old-school and emerging.

    We come from careers that have encompassed newspaper journalism and web development, magazine startups and coding, video and radio, ad agencies and art school. Which has prepared us perfectly for the multi-multi-multi-media world we live in now. Over the years, we’ve learned that people don’t want to be sold or told how to behave. They want to be connected with, talked and listened to and inspired – not just by stuff, but by ideas and people and the stories they tell. We’re a lot like that, too. In every medium.

Over the course of his career, Chris has led the branding and marketing of dozens of communities and destinations ranging from master planned destination resorts to entire cities.     WHAT

    STRATEGY

    Our team has led visioning, strategy and ideation workshops in many countries and in multiple languages. We get sparks – both creative and strategic – by rubbing brains together: our people and your people working as a team, because inspiration comes from places we don’t know by heart or by habit. We factor in the research we’ve done, and use all the combined energy to help you create rich, detailed visions of what the future could be like for your organization and your brand. The strategic documents that result are a clear, concise roadmap to your next successful product, property or destination.

    STORYTELLING

    We believe brands are given meaning through the stories we tell about them. Stories are a brand’s language and its lifeblood, its truth and its tone, informed by the dialect of clicks, Likes, Tweets and Yelps that the world speaks to talk about them. Engaging and authentic brand stories aren’t fluff that push a message out. They open a door and invite people in, connecting more deeply, satisfying curiosity, and making people feel good about the things they want to buy and the places that make them dream.

    DESIGN

    We believe that a brand has a pulse, that heart trumps head and that brand culture beats brand commoditization. That’s why the foundation of brand design is the emotional connection and unique, evocative language of a brand story. Together, storytelling and design bring the vision for a product, place or organization to vivid light. We’ve learned that without great design, a story is naked; without story, design is mere decoration.
