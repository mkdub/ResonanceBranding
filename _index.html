A story is an invitation. It opens a door and asks people in. A brand story is words and visuals that engage with emotion and inform in spades. Resonance Branding stories have the backbone of strategy, so they're smart, insightful and even transformative; they have a big, beating heart, so they can move people to think, to action or to tears. In our brand stories of products, places, companies and ideas, our clients are co-authors, main characters and heroes. The stories we write together - and send out into the world as narratives, logos, packaging, film, collateral or social media - are stories that define your company and articulate your vision: they build relationships, create value, change perceptions and advance your business.
